History

Ray Beauty Clinic was started in 1980 by Gwen Pietersma. Passion and professionalism is our hallmark. In order to achieve this the vision and aim of The Beauty Clinic is to render the best service possible and to constantly be at the forefront of skincare with new innovative products and updated training of all therapists. The highest standards of Hygiene are maintained at all times in spacious fully equipped treatment rooms.
